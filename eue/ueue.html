How To Install Mod

Open Melon playground and click FILE MANAGER

 

Click on the DOWNLOAD tab

 

Click BACK

 

 

Browse MELONMOD Folder

 

Choose folder mod that have you downloaded in this app

 

Click Mod that you want and click COPY TO MODS

 

 

Back to Melon Playground main screen, and click PLAY

 

Enjoy your MOD!!!
